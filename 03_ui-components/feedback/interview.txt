Feedback Components — Senior interview questions

1) How to design accessible dialogs and manage focus without leaking it?
- Answer: Implement focus trap, restore focus to trigger on close, use role="dialog" and aria-modal, and handle Escape and click-outside for dismissal.

2) How to architect toast systems for high-throughput UIs (many events)?
- Answer: Deduplicate messages, rate-limit displays, use aria-live polite for non-blocking announcements, and queue messages intelligently.

3) How to implement progress feedback for long-running background tasks?
- Answer: Use determinate progress when possible with aria-valuenow, or skeleton placeholders and incremental updates via web sockets or polling.

4) How to balance animation and accessibility for motion-sensitive users?
- Answer: Respect prefers-reduced-motion and provide static alternatives; reduce animation duration and avoid layout-shifting animations.

5) How to ensure tooltips are usable on touch devices?
- Answer: Provide alternate activation (taps), ensure tooltip content is reachable and not reliant on hover-only interactions.
