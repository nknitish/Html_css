What it is:
- Meta tags provide metadata — title, description, canonical, Open Graph — used by browsers and crawlers.

Why it matters:
- Important for SEO, social sharing, and mobile viewport behavior.

Key properties / syntax:
- <title>, <meta name="description">, <link rel="canonical">, Open Graph meta tags (og:*)

Interview questions:
1) Q: Why use canonical?
   A: To declare preferred URL to avoid duplicate content issues.
2) Q: Where to put meta tags?
   A: Inside the head.
3) Q: What is Open Graph used for?
   A: Controls preview when sharing on social platforms.
4) Q: Why set viewport?
   A: To control layout on mobile devices.
5) Q: Should title be unique per page?
   A: Yes.

Common gotchas:
- Missing description or duplicate titles.

Pro tips:
- Use server-rendered metadata or frameworks to keep metadata deterministic for SEO.
