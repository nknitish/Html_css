Keyboard-Accessible Example

Use only your keyboard on this page:

  • Tab / Shift+Tab to move focus
  • Enter or Space to activate buttons/links
  • Esc to close the modal

Try: Tab → focus "Open Modal" → press Enter → modal opens. Inside modal: Tab moves between Close, Cancel, Confirm. Press Esc to close.
